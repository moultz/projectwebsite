Introduction

There are three key deliverables that are crucical to the success of the website's redevelopment:

  1. Make the website responsive on desktop, tablet and mobile
  2. Moving from the Umbraco CMS to WordPress
  3. Updating and refreshing the website's content

These deliverables are further explorerd throughout the website.

It has been quite clear from the start that our ambition of the project to go beyond the website we develop just being a proof of concept. We wanted to create a website that was production worthy. This was acknowledged by our client and we have done our best to create the conditions that will enable us to deploy the website once we have finished the project.
